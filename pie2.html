Blueberry Pie Recipe

This Fresh home made treat filled with fresh berries is sure to pull the noses of all who live in the house like an old carton mouse

and this treat is great for bonding and reconnecting with those around you just bring a positive attitude and some butter!

Ingredients

  • Blue Blueberry(Of Course!)
  • Whipped Cream
  • Pie crust
  • Sugar
  • Blueberry Gelatin
  • pie tin

Steps

  1. dice the blueberries
  2. mash the blueberries
  3. form pie crust in a pie tin
  4. now pour the mashed blueberries in the tin with the pie crust
  5. mix blueberry gelatin and pour into pie crust
  6. take extra pie crust and cross hatch it along the top
  7. preheat oven to 250Â° and bake pie for 30-45 minutes
  8. apply whip cream to your liking

    9. and youre done! enjoy your yummy perfection
