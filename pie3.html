Apple Pie Recipe

This Fresh home made treat filled with fresh apples is sure to pull the noses of all who live in the house like an old carton mouse

and this treat is great for bonding and reconnecting with those around you just bring a positive attitude and some butter!

Ingredients

  • apples (Of Course!)
  • Whipped Cream
  • Pie crust
  • Sugar
  • apple Gelatin
  • pie tin

Steps

  1. dice the apples
  2. mash the apples
  3. form pie crust in a pie tin
  4. now pour the mashed apples in the tin with the pie crust
  5. mix apple gelatin and pour into pie crust
  6. take extra pie crust and cross hatch it along the top
  7. preheat oven to 250Â° and bake pie for 30-45 minutes
  8. apply whip cream to your liking

    9. and youre done! enjoy your yummy perfection
